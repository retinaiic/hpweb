Why Not Use a Realtor?

Main Photo Gallery Contact Arranging A Showing Realtor? To-Do List
  • Realtors charge 3-6% of the sale price of a home to broker the deal.
  • We'd like to keep that $20,000+ in our pockets, and those of the buyer!
  • If we can come to an agreement on the terms and conditions for the sale, our attorneys can work out the details, and we can save a lot of money.
  • If you think that a realtor would add value on your side of the deal, you are welcome to use one, and pay them for their services.
  • Check Zillow for current valuation. While it doesn't include some of the special features of this house, like copper gutters, and the woodwork, it gives a range of prices.
